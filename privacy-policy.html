Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 06, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Drawee collects minimal information necessary to provide our AR drawing services. We may collect:

• Device information (model, OS version) for compatibility

• Usage analytics to improve the app experience

• Camera access for AR drawing features (not stored or transmitted)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

• Provide and improve our AR drawing services

• Analyze app usage and performance

• Process subscription payments through Apple

3. Data Storage and Security

Your drawings and data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your drawings on our servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

• RevenueCat for subscription management

• Google AdMob for advertisements

• PostHog for analytics

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your information.

5. Camera and Photos Access

Drawee requires camera access to provide AR drawing functionality. Photos are accessed only to allow you to select images for tracing. We do not store, transmit, or share your photos or camera data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access your personal information

• Request deletion of your data

• Opt-out of analytics tracking

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
